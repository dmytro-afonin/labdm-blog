> **Thesis.** A lot of what shows up under the "cybersecurity" label is proof-of-work — visible effort that signals diligence to insurers, auditors, and customers — rather than measurable risk reduction. The mythos around it survives because nobody wants to be the person who removes a control and then gets breached.
